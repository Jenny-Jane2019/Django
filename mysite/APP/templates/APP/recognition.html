Operation Guide

Step one: Click the "upload" button on the left

This step is to upload your audio file. Only support MP3, MP4, wav format, and upload up to 10 files.


Step two: Click the "Audio File List" button on the left

This step is to show files you listed.And if you performed the previous step correctly and uploaded the file successfully, the web page will automatically jump to this page.


Step Three: Click the recognize button on the Audio File List page

This step is to realize online recognition. Detailed operation is to choose the audio you want to recognize in the audio file list and click the corresponding "recognition" button.


ps: Click the "Spectrum Map" button to view the spectrogram of all your uploaded audio files.
