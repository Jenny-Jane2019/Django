About data Set

Our data set includes a total of 40 speech command data of Chinese people in different regions, different age groups, and different genders. They recorded instructions at a distance of about 3m from the mobile phone in the room to simulate the scene of real instructions.

The collected commands include: turn on/off the air conditioner, turn on/off the light, increase/decrease the temperature, turn on/off the TV, cool/heat, increase/decrease the volume.
